What a gorgeous weekend it turned out to be! Even with some high clouds around this afternoon, we hit the low-70s. Unfortunately _every_ weekend in early spring can't be like this...but we'll sure take 'em when we can get 'em!

One more day tomorrow (Monday) of seasonably warm temperatures - back into the low- to mid-70s tomorrow afternoon. Potential for more high clouds tomorrow, which could affect temps by a couple degrees...but it'll still be another beautiful day!

Tuesday, temps drop back to around normal for this time of year - mid-50s. Slight chance for a brief shower or two, as a system scoots by to well our north...barely enough to wet the pavement if it happens though. Otherwise, we'll look to stay dry the rest of the week - temps should rebound back into the upper-50s to low-60s by late-week too!

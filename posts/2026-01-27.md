Our two-week dry-streak has officially come to an end! SeaTac had measurable precip just before 9pm, ending the streak at 14 days…just missing tying the 15 day mark by three hours.

The next few days (*finally*) bring a return to more normal weather for this time of year - periods of rain, some lightly-breezy winds, and more mild (above freezing) temps. Looking like around an inch of rain by the weekend, so not a complete washout.

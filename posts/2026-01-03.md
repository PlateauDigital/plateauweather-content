With the Seahawks locking down the #1 seed tonight, we can all take a breath! Thankfully, the weather should stay relatively normal for this time of year, to give our collective blood pressure and heart rates time to rest before the Divisional Round in two weeks.

Tomorrow (Sunday) will be a little wetter than today, but not a washout...also might be a bit breezy during the morning hours. Might see a few sunbreaks during the afternoon again! Monday looks fairly similar - showers and sunbreaks, though we'll start to turn a bit cooler too...afternoon highs will struggle to break out of the mid-40s. Another system makes its way to the area Tuesday, bringing more cold rain with it...and more of the same later in the week.

If you can't tell by now, chances for snow aren't looking good in the near/mid-term...and I'm not a fan of that. *However*, we do gain 60 minutes of daylight during the month of January, so there's that to look forward to!

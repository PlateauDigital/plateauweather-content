Tuesday night update: While we’re continuing to enjoy the dry break through the day tomorrow (Wednesday), it looks like it’ll be a little gusty as the next system approaches the coast. The National Weather Service has issued a Wind Advisory for 10am to 10pm Wednesday…and while it *technically* covers the Plateau, we’re on the very western edge of it. They’re wanting to call attention to places to our east - specifically North Bend - that will get gusts around 40 mph from the east. Around the Plateau, I’m not expecting much more than the 20-30 mph range. 

Honestly, it seems like a pretty standard “fall storm” for us - except for the fact we’re not expecting any rain during the day…normally, I wouldn’t make a big deal about it - but since we technically are under the advisory, I do want to pass it along. Tomorrow is also the one-year of the Bomb Cyclone, so there’s that too…

If March came "in like a lion cub" this year, I guess it's only fair that it goes out like a lamb! The workweek starts off fairly dry and pleasant, before turning wet again mid-week (no foolin' around here)...but we've gotta get through tonight (Sunday night) first.

As these showers wind down over the next couple hours, temps are already dropping into the mid- to upper-30s. There might be some "chunkiness" to the rain - it's not going to last long though. However, with temps dropping to near freezing overnight tonight into tomorrow (Monday) morning, car windows will be frosty, and roads could be a little slick early tomorrow morning.

Monday and Tuesday should be pretty nice! Chillier Monday, with afternoon highs in the upper-40s - a little warmer on Tuesday, bumping into the mid- to upper-50s. Should be at least partly-sunny throughout the days too!

The next system takes aim at us late Tuesday night into Wednesday. Looks like a wet, breezy day, with showers sticking around into Thursday...but an early look past that shows some drier signals for Easter weekend. More to come on both fronts in the next few days!

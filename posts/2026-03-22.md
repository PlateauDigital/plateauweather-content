For the first actual weekend of spring, can't really complain too much! One more nice day Monday to kick off the week, before a couple rainy, breezy days mid-week...but the end of the week seems to turn nice again!

Tomorrow (Monday) looks to be a pretty similar day to today - partly sunny through some high clouds, with highs in the low-50s.

The next system moves in Tuesday. Some light showers are possible ahead of it overnight Monday into Tuesday, before the bulk of the rain moves in during the morning hours. It'll be pretty wet during the day, turning to showers by Tuesday evening. Looks like a windy day too - but it's worth calling out that this should _not_ be like storm a couple weeks ago. The center of the system is forecast to be well to our north (near the northern tip of Vancouver Island), further away than the last storm - meaning we shouldn't get the strong winds we did last time. It will get pretty gusty though!

Showers continue Wednesday into Thursday morning, before things look to dry out and warm up into the weekend. We'll take a closer look at that later this week. Back tomorrow with an update on Tuesday's forecast!

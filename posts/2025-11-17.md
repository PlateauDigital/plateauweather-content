For as dreary as it’s been the past several days, it’s been…kinda boring? The November mist has set in! Much of the activity has been well to our south, with much of the greater Los Angeles area getting over 2 inches of rain over the weekend!

Back around these parts, we’ll continue our ho-hum weather for a few days! Scattered showers, partly-sunny skies, and afternoon high temps in the low-50s. With all the moisture we’ve gotten recently, along with the partial clearing, we’ll most likely wake up to fog the next couple mornings. Light rain chances increase again Thursday thru the weekend, as a few systems brush past.

Sunday night update: wet and windy starting tomorrow (Monday) morning.

Rain starts picking up during the morning commute, and will fall pretty steadily through the day - could be as much as an inch by tomorrow night. 

It’ll also be windy throughout the day, and looks a tad stronger than Friday. The National Weather Service has issued a Wind Advisory this time, from 7am until 10pm - calling for winds from the south at 15-25 mph, gusting up to 45 mph. I think the Plateau will most likely get into the upper 30 mph range…the peak looks to be mid-day in early afternoon, though it’ll be plenty gusty before and after that.

Another update tomorrow morning, including a look at the rest of the week!

Cool, showery start to the week - and to December! - but overall, a relatively boring week weather-wise...though indications show things turn active by the end of the week again.

At 8pm Sunday night, my backyard is already below freezing for the second night in a row! Cloud cover will increase overnight, which should keep the temps from falling too much further. Chance of a couple showers tomorrow morning around daybreak - if temps are still cool enough, can't rule out a few "chunky raindrops" mixing in, if you know what I mean... absolutely nothing worth getting hopes up over though.

Shower chances continue throughout the day Monday and Tuesday, but total amounts look very light. Wednesday looks dry, before shower chances return towards the end of the week. Afternoon high temps don't look to make it much higher than the mid- to upper-40s throughout the week, while overnight lows bottom out in the mid-30s.
